The West Sussex Beekeepers’ Association is again organising a Bee Market and Auction for the benefit of its members and non-members. The event will again take place at Chichester College's Brinsbury Campus in Pulborough, West Sussex on Saturday 25th April 2026.  Although it cannot be guaranteed we already have a number of Lots of honey bee nucs registered for the auction sale.

Auction details, Guidance Notes and Entry Form are available to download from the WSBKA website - [www.westsussexbeekeepers.org.uk](https://www.westsussexbeekeepers.org.uk/auction.html)    If any Association would like hard copies of the Guidance Notes and/or Entry Form please let me know.  The Auction catalogue will be published online on or after 20th April.

We look forward to seeing as many of your members as possible at Brinsbury.
